Chaugan Player

Details

SUBJECT MATTER :- This painting is a horizontal painting in which two princesses are playing polo with their four maids.
DESCRIPTION:- The princesses are seated on back of two different horses in the upper section of the painting and are facing each other. The other four horses in the lower section of the painting are running in opposite direction. The four maids who are riding it are facing the same side the princesses are looking. All characters are perfected with red, orange, ultramarine and mauve colors. The polo stick held by all six character in the painting is covering the ball.

The ladies have high rounded foreheads. Their faces are profile. their eyes are elongated in shape of lotus buds up to their themples.The skirts of all the female figures is spread on the horse back like bells in a stylised manner.

The composition is against flat green background. Bright colors are used to bring boldness in the painting. The light green background plays a contrast to the painting subject. All the dresses are in contemporary Rajput costumes and well ornamented.

HUMAN VALUES:
1.Sports as a recreation for healthy and fulfilling life.
2.Teamwork towards a common goal.
3.A sense of healty competition to get better in every field.
4.Spirit of friendship.

NAME:- CHAUGAN PLAYERS

ARTIST:- DANA

MEDIUM:- TEMPERA (water color) ON PAPER

SUB-SCHOOL:- JODHPUR,RAJASTHAN

COLLECTION:- THE NATIONAL MUSEUM, NEW DELHI
