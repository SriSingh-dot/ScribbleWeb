Krishna on Swing

Details

SUBJECT MATTER :-This is a painting showcasing a scene from Ramayana where Bharat has came to Chitrakut to meet Ram with his three mothers a guru vashisth. The painting is in continuous narration as you can see repeatition of characters at different positions. Various episodes has been shown in a single painting DESCRIPTION:- In the background different shades of green has been used very skillfully throughout the painting. Ram,Sita and Lakshman stand out against the freen lavish forest. Even the foreground and background of hut is coloured in green. A grove of lush green bush and trees has been painted behind the hut.
This small painting consist of total 49 figures from left to right. The story begins from left side of the painting where ladies of the family are coming towards hut to meet Ram, Sita and Lakshman. Sita kneels down to greet mothers. Kaushalya hugs her son Ram in grief and guilt and later Ram sits and meet with the sages. The sages inform him about his father's death and on hearing Ram faints. The mothers, the three wives of princes and Sita are seen sitting together and talking to each other. With all figures exiting the scene, the narration ends at the right side of the painting. On top of every scene one could see labels written in small font.
In the painting earthy colors are used. The white colour in the painting stands out showing to movement of characters from left to right. White color creates movement in the composition. Ram has been shown in blueish-black with a halo and Sita has been shown with a tinge of red on her colthes. At the time of this painting, the mughal influences had receded and Jaipur style came into prominence.

HUMAN VALUES:
1.Giving Forgiveness should be a quality in one being (Even Lord Ram forgave Kaushalya).
2.Devotion and love between family members.
3.Respect for all those older than us.
4.One should follow the direction of their parents.
5.To be patient to listen to advice of people older than us as well as wiser than us.
6.One should sacrifice their own comfort for their loved ones.
7.One should not do things that they might regret later.
