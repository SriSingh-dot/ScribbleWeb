Miniature Paintings

History of Miniature Painting

Any painting done in small size on any surface, medium or plane is called "Miniature Painting".Earlier in India they were made on palm leaves,a piece of cloth or a piece of leather.After years,it was drawn on handmade paper.Even after being mini in size, the paintings were presented with minute details.These were mainly portraits,an illustration of a story or a painting inspired from daily life of a man. From medieval times Indian Art schools were higly influenced by miniature paintings.
